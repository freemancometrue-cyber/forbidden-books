# Forbidden Books Archive

Welcome to the Forbidden Books Archive. This repository collects numerous books that have been banned from publication, distribution, or subjected to strict censorship in mainland China due to political, historical, or cultural reasons.

These books are like starlight in the dark night, recording the true history obscured by official grand narratives, reflecting the profound introspection of intellectuals on the nation's destiny, and the real encounters of people at the bottom during turbulent times. Whether it's restoring the truth of the CCP's history, recording the bitter lessons of the Great Famine and the Cultural Revolution, or exposing the political games at the center of power, these forbidden books are our important channels to seek the truth and refuse to forget.

Reading these books is not only a adherence to freedom of speech and thought, but also an awakening of the spirit of independent thinking. We have compiled detailed book lists, authors, and core themes for you, dedicated to building a free reading and communication space, so that those memories that should not be erased can be inherited.
